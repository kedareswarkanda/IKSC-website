Welcome to IKSC Student Chapter – KARE

Our Vision

To inspire, connect, and empower students of KARE to become future-ready leaders, innovators, and changemakers who contribute meaningfully to society through knowledge, collaboration, and creativity.

Our Mission

Learning Culture

Foster a culture of learning, innovation, and community engagement.

Skill Development

Provide a platform for students to develop technical, professional, and leadership skills.

Networking

Build strong connections between students, industry experts, and alumni for career growth.

Core Values

Innovation

Encouraging new ideas, creative problem-solving, and forward thinking.

Collaboration

Working together to achieve shared goals and mutual growth.

Integrity

Upholding honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all actions.

Excellence

Striving for high standards in academics, projects, and personal development.

Inclusivity

Creating an open environment where diversity is respected and celebrated.

Community Service

Giving back to society through impactful initiatives and volunteer work.
